BRIONES VISITS CITY HIGH AFTER PISA FEAT
January 31, 2020

Department of Education Secretary Leonor Magtolis-Briones visited Baguio City National High School to personally commend the school for being the country’s top scorer in the reading comprehension results of the 2018 Programme for International Student Assessment (PISA).

“I have not come here to teach, but to learn about the teaching practices of the Baguio City National High School,” Briones said during the school’s welcome program for the secretary on January 31.

Prior to her visit to the school, Briones attended the launching of DepEd CAR’s Project READ to LEAD in Wangal, La Trinidad where Mrs. Brenda M. Cariño, Secondary School Principal IV, received the certificate of recognition from Regional Director May B. Eclar and Assistant Regional Director Florante E. Vergara.
